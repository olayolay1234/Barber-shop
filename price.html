Our Barber Services Explore our variety of haircuts and styles tailored to fit your unique look. Below are some of the most popular styles we offer.

1. Classic Fade Price: ¥25 Description: A timeless cut where the hair gradually fades from short to longer. Perfect for those who want a clean, professional look while maintaining some length on top. Ideal for any occasion.

2. Buzz Cut Price: ¥15 Description: A simple, low-maintenance cut that uses clippers to give you a uniform, short length all over the scalp. Great for men who prefer a clean, sharp look without much upkeep.

3. Pompadour Price: ¥30 Description: The pompadour is a bold, vintage-inspired style where the hair is swept back and elevated, creating a voluminous and smooth appearance. This style adds flair and suits most face shapes.

4. Undercut Price: ¥28 Description: Featuring shorter sides with longer hair on top, the undercut creates a striking contrast. This versatile style can be worn sleek or messy for a more modern, edgy appearance.

5. Crew Cut Price: ¥22 Description: A short, tapered style that’s neat and easy to maintain. The hair is cut short on the sides and top, making it perfect for active individuals who need a practical, low-maintenance look.

6. High and Tight Price: ¥20 Description: Popular with military personnel, this style features very short sides and a slightly longer top. It’s a clean, sharp look that’s perfect for those who want a no-fuss style.

7. Taper Fade Price: ¥25 Description: A gradual fade from short to long hair that creates a seamless blend between the hair on top and the sides. This cut is versatile and can be customized based on your personal preferences.

8. Quiff Price: ¥30 Description: The quiff combines volume and style, where the hair is styled up and back for a more polished, classic appearance. This look is perfect for those wanting a dapper, sophisticated style.

9. Caesar Cut Price: ¥23 Description: A short, sharp cut where the bangs are cut horizontally across the forehead. The Caesar cut is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish and structured look.

10. Mohawk Price: ¥35 Description: A bold and edgy style where the hair is shaved on the sides, leaving a strip of longer hair running from front to back. Perfect for individuals who love standing out and making a statement.
