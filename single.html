Privacy policy


At Beard & Blade, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services, visit our website, or interact with us. By visiting our barbershop or using our services, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.


1. Information We Collect We collect personal information from you in various ways, including when you book appointments, visit our website, or contact us directly. The information we collect includes: Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when booking an appointment. Payment Information: Credit/debit card details or payment method information for processing transactions. Service Preferences: Information about the services you receive or request, including any preferences or allergies that may be relevant to your service. Website Usage Information: If you visit our website, we may collect data about your browsing behavior, such as IP addresses, browser type, and how you interact with our website using cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect for the following purposes: To process and confirm your appointments and transactions. To communicate with you regarding your appointments, promotions, and special offers. To improve the services we offer, including personalization of your experience at our barbershop. To respond to inquiries, feedback, and requests for information. To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and conditions.


3. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations: Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors) who assist us in running our business and providing services to you. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or other legal process. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction, and we will notify you if your personal information is transferred to a different privacy policy.


4. Data Security We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmitting data over the internet or storing data is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.


5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, track website traffic, and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie settings through your web browser, but disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our website.


6. Retention of Data We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.


7. Your Rights You have the right to: Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. Correction: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information. Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions. Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.


8. Children’s Privacy We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible.


9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
