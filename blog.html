Term & condition


1. Appointment Booking:

All appointments should be booked in advance through our website, phone, or walk-in (subject to availability). A valid credit card is required to book certain services or appointments. If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please provide at least 24 hours’ notice to avoid cancellation fees.


2. Cancellation Policy:

If you cancel or reschedule an appointment less than 24 hours in advance, a cancellation fee of [amount] will be charged. No-shows or missed appointments will incur a charge of [amount] or [percentage] of the scheduled service price.


3. Payment:

Payment is due at the time of service. We accept payment via cash, debit/credit card, and other forms as determined by the barbershop. Gratuities for services rendered are appreciated but not required:


4. Service Satisfaction:

We aim to provide high-quality services, but if you are not satisfied with your service, please notify us within 48 hours so we can make things right. We do not provide refunds for services rendered, but we may offer a follow-up service if necessary.


5. Health & Safety:

All barbers adhere to strict hygiene and safety practices, including sanitizing tools and workstations after each client. If you have any health conditions, allergies, or concerns, please inform your barber prior to the service. If you feel unwell or have symptoms of any contagious illnesses, we kindly ask that you reschedule your appointment.


6. Conduct:

We ask all clients to behave respectfully and courteously toward our staff and other clients. Any inappropriate behavior may result in the termination of the service. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone displaying disruptive behavior or violating our terms and conditions.


7. Children & Minors:

Minors under the age of 16 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian during their appointment. A parent or guardian must provide consent for any service on a minor under the age of 18.


8. Personal Belongings:

The barbershop is not responsible for lost or stolen personal items. Please keep all personal belongings with you during your visit.

9. Privacy & Data:

We respect your privacy. Any personal information collected for appointments or promotions will be used solely for business purposes and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.


10. Right to Modify:

The barbershop reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website or communicated directly to clients.
